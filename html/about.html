About Climate Map

This map shows climate normals, such as temperature and precipitation amounts, as colours on the map.

Click anywhere on the map to see charts on the local climate.

Drag the slider at the bottom to see the forecast for the future climate.

You can change the climate model and period in the menu on the left.

Citations and links to the original data are shown at the bottom of the screen.

Climate is not weather. This map does NOT show the current weather.

Caveats

The author of this app takes NO responsibility for damages or injuries caused by trusting the information presented in the app. Errors are absolutely possible and inevitable.

Historical data here are interpolated from station data from CRU. Local data may be based on a weather station or it may not be. Check your local government's climate data to be safe.

Projections from the models are calibrated against the historical data in order to compare apples to apples. The forecasted normals show the projected changes rather than what the models think the climate normals are going to be. The process was inspired by WorldClim's downscaling algorithm.
