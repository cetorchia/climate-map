This map shows climate normals, such as average temperature and average precipitation, as colours on the map.

Climate is not weather. If weather is like a person's mood, then climate is like their personality.

Climate data is approximated in some cases and may differ from actual climate normals. Temperatures and precipitation amounts can change within short distances. Therefore the average temperature and precipitation for a region may not represent all places in the region.
